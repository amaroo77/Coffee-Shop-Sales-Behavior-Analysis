## 5. Observations
- The **morning period (7‚Äì10 AM)** is the most active and profitable time of the day.  
- Customers tend to favor **premium and freshly brewed drinks**, indicating a preference for quality over quantity.  
- **Chai-based and espresso drinks** outperform other categories, showing strong customer interest in both traditional and strong-flavor beverages.  
- Non-peak hours (after 10 AM) have lower sales, suggesting potential underutilized capacity during midday and afternoon.

---

### A. Revenue Growth Strategies
1. **Morning Promotions:**  
   Launch exclusive ‚ÄúMorning Combo Deals‚Äù (e.g., coffee + pastry) to encourage early visits and repeat purchases.  
2. **Loyalty Programs:**  
   Offer reward points or discounts for customers who visit consistently during morning hours.  

### B. Operational Optimization
3. **Staff Allocation:**  
   Increase barista and cashier coverage during 7‚Äì10 AM to maintain service speed and quality.  
4. **Inventory Management:**  
   Prioritize stock levels for top-performing beverages and ingredients during morning hours to prevent shortages.  

### C. Marketing Initiatives
5. **Targeted Advertising:**  
   Run morning-themed marketing campaigns such as *‚ÄúStart Your Day Right‚Äù* or *‚ÄúYour Morning Ritual Awaits‚Äù* on social media.

### D. Off-Peak Monetization
7. **Afternoon Promotions:**  
   Offer discounts or ‚ÄúBuy 1 Free 1‚Äù deals after 2 PM to boost foot traffic during slow hours.
